My Flora
(Tokushima to Tokyo)

Fee: 12000JPY/90USD
Route: Tokushima - Tokyo
Bus: Kaifu Bus - My Flora
Type: My flora capsule.

The night highway bus "My Flora" of the 'Kaifu Kanko' connects Tokyo and Tokushima (Shikoku island). "My Flora" is very famous because only 12 seats can be comfortably placed in the car. The seats are partitioned one by one by partitions made of wood from Tokushima prefecture, and if you close the curtains, you can rest almost like a private room. There is a powder room at the rear of the vehicle, which can be used for changing clothes and makeup. Amenity (pile slippers, hand towels), small TVs installed in each seat, outlets, etc., is the highest quality car that pursues comfortable movement to the maximum. It’s a 9.5-hour bus trip, but you can have super relax time.
